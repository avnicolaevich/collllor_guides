You have the order!

When an order is placed, you will be notified via email. Click the link in your Invoice Email and open the orders’ page in your account. New orders are highlighted.

1. Inspect Files

Review submitted files. Wrong order? Contact your buyer ASAP. You can also cancel the order by clicking a grey status bar under the word “Canceled”

2. Accept Order or Send an Approval

After receiving the files move your order into “In Production” by clicking “I Accept Order”. If your buyer requested electronic approval you will be offered to upload files and send them for final approval. After your buyer approves the files, the order automatically moves into “In Production”

3. Shipping & Reviews

Change the status of the order to “Shipped” by sending the tracking number to your customer.

In the same window, send review request to your buyer in a number of days you think is reasonable.

Payment

We make payments right after your order was shipped.

It takes up to 1-5 business days for the bank transfer, check, or Paypal transfer. 7% are taken from the order, including additional services cost. Tax and shipping cost are not included.

Good Image Sells

Every product on Collllor needs 1-4 images. Choose images that are clear, easy to understand, information-rich, and attractively presented. Images must accurately represent the product and show only the product you sell, with minimal or no propping. Size: 1000 pixels or larger in either height or width, JPEG (.jpg), up to 1MB, landscape format preferred. View Image Requirements
